Welcome to "Better Than Nothing." Play only if you're REALLY bored! :)


Rules:

  • This game plays in your console. Open it up!
  • You have to roll each die once per game. Once you roll it, it'll disappear.
  • Your goal is to roll 42. If the sum of your rolls is between 39-42, you win!
  • If you roll exactly 42, you REALLY win, and you get an awesome surprise!
  • If you roll over 42, you fail.
  • If you roll less than 39, you REALLY fail, and you get a terrible surprise.
  • When you've used all your rolls, click the final button!
  • Good luck!
